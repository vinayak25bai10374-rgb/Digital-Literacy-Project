5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student.*Doís for Responsible Social Media Use* Think before you post and ensure your content is respectful and appropriate. * Protect your privacy by using strong passwords and proper privacy settings. * Verify information before sharing it with others. * Use social media for learning, networking, and sharing useful knowledge. * Maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects your personality and values. *Doníts for Responsible Social Media Use* Donít share sensitive personal information like your address, phone number, or passwords. * Donít post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content. * Donít spread rumors or fake news without checking reliable sources. * Donít spend excessive time on social media that affects your studies. * Donít engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying.
